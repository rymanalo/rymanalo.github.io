About

Hello! My name is Ryan Manalo. I graduated from UC Berkeley in May 2011 with a BA in Cognitive Science. After working for about a year in sales at the California Culinary Academy, I yearned for a change in my life towards a more fulfilling, passion-filled career. I was ecstatic to hear that I was accepted into General Assembly's Web Development Immersive Program. This page will give you a little taste of all that I have learned. Check out my projects and feel free to connect with me!

Projects

Club 1000Lbs is a weightlifting tracker app. Everyday you do a workout, you can input new data. You can see your progress as you try to reach Club 1000lbs.

GoodFood is designed to be a mobile app that searches for and informs you of restaurants near you that have a Yelp rating of 4 stars or more.

Eargasm compiles a biography, picture, and YouTube playlist of top tracks for any music artist you search for all in one page.
